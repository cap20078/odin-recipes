UPMA


Ingredients required:

  • Rava
  • Water
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Potato
  • Chilly
  • Cumin seeds
  • Salt

Procedure:

First roast the rava in a pan until it turns into brown colour.Boil the water in another container with all vegetables chopped in it.After boiling the water pour the rava into it with continous stirring to avoid lumps.Add cumin seeds,salt for taste.Let it to be settled for 15 minutes. Then turn off the gas and leave it for ten minutes.

Now the Upma is ready to serve.Serve it with any chatni for better taste.


Happy cooking!
